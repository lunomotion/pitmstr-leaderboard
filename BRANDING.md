## Design Principles

- Admin portal uses **Americana Blue** gradient sidebar with white text
- Public-facing pages lean on **BBQ Red** and **Smoke Black**
- Cards use white backgrounds with subtle borders (`#E5E5E5`)
- Rounded corners: `rounded-xl` (cards), `rounded-lg` (buttons), `rounded-full` (badges)
- Icons: Lucide React throughout
- Mobile-first responsive design
